## 3. Serious Incident Reporting (Article 73)

A serious incident must be reported to market surveillance authorities without undue delay and no later than 15 days after the provider becomes aware.

A serious incident is any incident that directly or indirectly leads to, or is likely to lead to:
- Death or serious damage to health
- Serious and irreversible disruption of critical infrastructure management
- Breach of fundamental rights

### Reporting procedure:
1. Immediate assessment of incident severity
2. Notification to market surveillance authority within 15 days
3. Full investigation report within timeframe set by authority
4. Corrective measures implementation
5. Follow-up reporting until resolved

# Prohibited AI Practices (Article 5)

Your AI system falls under a prohibited category. It **cannot be placed on the EU market or put into service in the EU**.

## What to do

1. **Stop deployment** in the EU immediately
2. **Assess alternatives** that achieve the same goal without falling under prohibitions
3. **Check for narrow exceptions** (some law enforcement uses have limited exceptions under strict conditions)
4. **Consult legal counsel** specializing in EU AI Act compliance

## Penalties for violation

- Up to **35 million EUR** or **7% of global annual turnover**, whichever is higher

## Prohibited since: February 2, 2025

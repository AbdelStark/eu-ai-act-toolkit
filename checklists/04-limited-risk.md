# Limited Risk AI System Compliance Checklist

Your AI system has transparency obligations under Article 50 of the EU AI Act.

---

## Transparency Requirements

### If your system interacts with natural persons (chatbots, voice assistants):
- [ ] Users are informed they are interacting with an AI system (unless obvious from context)
- [ ] Disclosure is clear, timely, and in an accessible format

### If your system generates synthetic content (deepfakes, AI text/images/audio/video):
- [ ] Output is marked as artificially generated or manipulated
- [ ] Marking is machine-readable where technically feasible
- [ ] Exception: content that is part of an obviously creative, satirical, artistic, or fictional work (but still must not mislead about real events)

### If your system performs emotion recognition or biometric categorization:
- [ ] Persons exposed to the system are informed of its operation
- [ ] Personal data is processed in accordance with GDPR and relevant data protection law

### If your system generates or manipulates text published to inform the public on matters of public interest:
- [ ] The fact that the content has been AI-generated or manipulated is disclosed

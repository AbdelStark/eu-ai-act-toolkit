# Minimal Risk AI System

Your AI system does not fall under any regulated category. There are no legal obligations under the EU AI Act.

## Voluntary Measures (Encouraged)

While not legally required, the EU AI Act encourages minimal-risk AI providers to:

- [ ] Voluntarily apply the requirements for high-risk systems (or a subset)
- [ ] Adopt voluntary codes of conduct, including regarding environmental sustainability, accessibility, stakeholder participation, and diversity of development teams
- [ ] Follow the AI Pact commitments (voluntary early adoption of certain AI Act provisions)

## Good Practices

Even without legal obligation, consider:
- Documenting your system's capabilities and limitations
- Monitoring for unexpected behaviors in deployment
- Being transparent with users about AI involvement
- Maintaining data quality standards

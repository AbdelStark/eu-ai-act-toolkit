# GPAI with Systemic Risk Compliance Checklist

Your model poses systemic risk under the EU AI Act (Article 51 + 55). This applies if your model was trained with more than 10^25 FLOPs of compute, or has been designated by the EU AI Office.

**Enforcement date: August 2, 2025**

---

## All GPAI obligations (from Article 51-53):

- [ ] Technical documentation (Annex XI)
- [ ] Information for downstream providers
- [ ] Copyright compliance
- [ ] Training data summary

## Additional systemic risk obligations (Article 55):

- [ ] Perform model evaluation in accordance with standardised protocols and tools
  - [ ] Including adversarial testing (red teaming) to identify and mitigate systemic risks
- [ ] Assess and mitigate possible systemic risks, including their sources
- [ ] Track, document, and report serious incidents and possible corrective measures to the AI Office and relevant national authorities without undue delay
- [ ] Ensure an adequate level of cybersecurity protection for the model and its physical infrastructure
